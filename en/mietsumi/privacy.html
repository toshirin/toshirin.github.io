Privacy

MieTsumi Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 23, 2026

Our approach

The developer of MieTsumi respects the privacy of its users.

This policy explains how information is handled in MieTsumi.

1. Information collected by the app

MieTsumi does not collect personal information or usage data through use of the app.

The app does not use:

  • User accounts
  • Advertising
  • Behavioral analytics
  • Analytics SDKs
  • Custom usage-log transmission
  • Automatic crash-report transmission
  • Location data
  • The microphone
  • The camera
  • Transmission of block models to an external server

2. Block data

Block arrangements, colors, and display settings created in the app are stored on the user’s device and are not sent to an external server.

If cloud synchronization is added in the future, this policy will be updated.

3. Photo library

When the user runs the “Save to Photos” feature, an image of the current block model is saved to the device’s photo library.

Saving occurs only after an explicit action by the user.

Saved images are not sent to the developer or an external server.

4. In-app purchases

The Full Version is planned to use Apple’s App Store in-app purchase system.

Payment information is processed by Apple.

The developer does not receive credit card numbers or other payment details.

The app may refer to purchase-status information provided by Apple to determine whether purchased features are available.

5. Contact messages

When a user contacts the developer by email, the sender’s email address, subject, message, and any attachments are delivered to the developer.

This information is used only to respond to the inquiry and investigate reported issues.

It is not provided to third parties without consent, except when required by law.

Information that is no longer needed beyond the period required to handle the inquiry will be deleted at an appropriate time.

6. External pages and services

The app or support site may open an email app, the App Store, or an external web page.

Information handled by an external service is governed by that service’s privacy policy.

This support site does not use independent analytics, advertising, or cookie-based tracking.

7. Use by children

MieTsumi is a learning app intended for children to use together with a parent or guardian.

The app does not provide a feature for entering or sending a child’s name, age, face photo, voice, or location.

Purchases and support inquiries should be handled by a parent or guardian.

8. Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated when app features, laws, or related services change.

Important changes will be announced by updating this page.

9. Contact

For questions about this policy, contact:
